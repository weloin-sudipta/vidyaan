# Publication Workflow

## Overview
Content approval and publication workflow for notices, announcements, and school communications ensuring quality control and appropriate dissemination.

## Workflow Stages

### 1. Content Creation
**Actors:** Teachers, Administrators, Students (limited)
**Purpose:** Develop clear, appropriate, and effective communication content

#### Process Steps:
1. **Content Planning**
   - Identify communication purpose and audience
   - Choose appropriate content type and format
   - Define key messages and call-to-actions

2. **Draft Development**
   - Write clear, concise content
   - Include relevant details and deadlines
   - Ensure appropriate tone and language

3. **Attachment Preparation**
   - Gather supporting documents
   - Format attachments appropriately
   - Verify file sizes and compatibility

#### Content Guidelines:
- Clear and specific subject lines
- Actionable information with deadlines
- Appropriate reading level for audience
- Inclusive and respectful language
- Mobile-friendly formatting

### 2. Internal Review Process
**Actors:** Content Creator, Department Heads
**Purpose:** Ensure content accuracy, appropriateness, and alignment

#### Process Steps:
1. **Self-Review**
   - Grammar and spelling check
   - Fact verification
   - Content completeness validation

2. **Department Review**
   - Subject matter expert validation
   - Department head approval
   - Alignment with school policies

3. **Quality Assurance**
   - Content appropriateness check
   - Format and presentation review
   - Technical requirement verification

#### Review Criteria:
- Accuracy of information
- Appropriateness for target audience
- Compliance with school policies
- Clarity and readability
- Technical quality of attachments

### 3. Approval Workflow
**Actors:** Department Heads, Administrators
**Purpose:** Authorize content publication based on organizational hierarchy

#### Process Steps:
1. **Initial Approval Routing**
   - Automatic routing based on content type
   - Escalation to appropriate approval authority
   - Parallel approval for multi-department content

2. **Approval Review**
   - Content quality assessment
   - Policy compliance verification
   - Resource and budget impact review

3. **Final Authorization**
   - Administrative sign-off
   - Publication scheduling approval
   - Distribution channel authorization

#### Approval Matrix:
- **Routine Notices:** Department Head
- **Academic Announcements:** Academic Coordinator
- **Emergency Communications:** Principal/Director
- **Policy Changes:** School Administration
- **External Communications:** Communications Director

### 4. Publication Scheduling
**Actors:** Administrators, System
**Purpose:** Time publication for maximum effectiveness

#### Process Steps:
1. **Timing Optimization**
   - Consider audience availability
   - Account for time zone differences
   - Align with school calendar events

2. **Channel Selection**
   - Choose appropriate communication channels
   - Consider audience preferences
   - Plan multi-channel distribution

3. **Scheduling Execution**
   - Set publication timestamps
   - Configure automated distribution
   - Prepare contingency plans

#### Timing Considerations:
- Academic year cycle awareness
- Holiday and break period adjustments
- Peak attention time identification
- Follow-up communication planning

### 5. Content Distribution
**Actors:** System, Administrators
**Purpose:** Deliver content through appropriate channels effectively

#### Process Steps:
1. **Digital Distribution**
   - Portal and app notifications
   - Email and SMS broadcasts
   - Social media posting

2. **Physical Distribution**
   - Notice board posting
   - Handout distribution
   - Physical mail for special cases

3. **Verification Process**
   - Delivery confirmation checking
   - Read receipt monitoring
   - Follow-up for critical communications

#### Distribution Channels:
- **Student Portal:** Primary digital channel
- **Parent App:** Mobile notifications
- **Email System:** Formal communications
- **SMS Service:** Urgent notifications
- **Physical Boards:** Traditional backup

### 6. Post-Publication Management
**Actors:** Administrators, System
**Purpose:** Monitor effectiveness and handle follow-up

#### Process Steps:
1. **Engagement Monitoring**
   - Read rate and engagement tracking
   - Response and action monitoring
   - Feedback collection and analysis

2. **Issue Resolution**
   - Clarification requests handling
   - Content correction if needed
   - Follow-up communication planning

3. **Performance Analysis**
   - Communication effectiveness measurement
   - Channel performance comparison
   - Continuous improvement insights

#### Analytics Tracking:
- Open rates and read times
- Response rates and actions taken
- Audience engagement patterns
- Channel effectiveness comparison
